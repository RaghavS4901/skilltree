About the platform

We are a small dedicated team, trying to solve the problems of HR industry

Skill Enhancement

Either individually, or in a group complete the course which you want to within a stipulated period of time. After that, in an interactive quiz format, attempt the questions related to course content, in the end, top 10-20% scorers get an exclusive course completion certificate

Internships and Competitions

Companies can easily list internships opportunities and competitions. Job seekers and other people can easily apply to those internships and competitions and gain experience, which they can later add to their own resume and cv, this gives them an edge over other job seekers and improves their knowledge at the same time as well.

Integrated Resume and CV

Thanks to our integrated resume and cv builder, every course you attend, every competition you enter, every internship you get, and every certificate you attain gets automatically entered here. A job recruiter looking at your profile can also look at the integrated resume and can see how much experience you have attained till now. Moreover, you can integrate this inbuilt resume with your actual resume and increase your chances of getting noticed by other professionals.

Social Media Platform

With SKILLTREE SOCIAL MEDIA platform, you can connect with professionals and directly approach them for mentorship, collaboration or even for a job interview. Job professionals can find skilled and experienced people on this platform.

Chat Room

Through our chat platform, you can find like minded individuals with whom you can create lifelong relations, furthermore there would be regular Ask Me Anything sessions on the community platform which would be taken by skilled and successful entrepreneurs and people from all fields.

Metaverse Based Hiring Platform

We are currently in the process of building an interactive metaverse based platform, which would be an alternative to video call apps such as zoom, meet, etc and professional can easily conduct online interviews here, startups from any location can directly pitch to investors here. The possibilities on what can be done here are limitless. The only limit is your imagination.

How It Works

We are a team of dedicated creators working on solving the major issues pertaining in HR industry.

Step 1

Job seekers, service providers, and job recruiters register on the platform.

Step 2

People participate in various skill enhancement courses over the platform, and play a short and interactive quiz after successfully completing their course. In the end, the top scorers get exclusive completion certificates.

Step 3

You can also participate in various internships, scholarships and competitions, and top scorers win exclusive certificate of merit which not only shows your experience but skills as well.

Step 4

Attend as much events as you can and add to your resume and cv, both of which play an integral role in getting proffessionals to notice you. It is the resume, skills and experience which gives you an edge over other job seekers.

Step 5

Network with industry professionals, go through job listings on the platform, get noticed by winning competitions, internship opportunities and show them your skills and experience.

Step 6

Get selected for Interviews, give your best shot and ultimately make a living in the field which you love and ace at.

Step 7

Give us a positive feedback on play store, app store, and follow us on our social media handles and reccomend to your friends and family members :)
